Fettucine Alfredo

A Brief Description of Fettucine Alfredo:

Fettucine Alfredo is a creamy, cheesy pasta dish.

You can't really beat that.

Ingredients:

  • Fettucine Pasta (or preferred pasta style)
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan
  • Black Pepper
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt
  • Chicken (optional)

Steps to create the dish:

  1. Bring water to a boil with a half tablespoon or so of salt added to it
  2. Once water is boiling, add your noodles and cook til preferred texture
  3. Remove your garlic from its skin and mince it finely
  4. Grate your parmesan as finely as your grater allows
  5. Heat a sautee pan on medium heat and add your preferred oil or butter
  6. Once appropriately heated, toss in your minced garlic and sautee until the sharper garlic fragrance dies and you're left with that wonderful cooked garlic smell
  7. Add in your heavy cream to your garlic and oil
  8. Once the cream starts to bubble, add in your grated parmesan, reserving some for garnish
  9. Stir the forming sauce until combined and smooth
  10. Crank in some fresh black pepper and be sure to taste your sauce, add salt and pepper as needed
  11. Toss cooked pasta into your sauce and serve with a sprinkle of reserved parmesan on top!
  12. Optionally: top pasta with grilled chicken if you would like a protein
