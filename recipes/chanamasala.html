Chana Masala

A Brief Description of Chana Masala:

Chana Masala is a rich and flavorful Indian curry.

Caramelized onions and tomatoes make the curry's

base, which coupled with the flavor packed spice blend,

this dish is sure to warm your soul.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Chickpeas
  • Masala Spice Blend
  • Water or Veggie Stock
  • Fresh Cilantro (optional)
  • Fresh Lime (optional)

Steps to create the dish:

  1. Dice your onions
  2. Peel your ginger and remove your garlic from its skin
  3. Blend your tomatoes, garlic and ginger into a puree
  4. Caramelize your onions until they're deeply browned
  5. Pour the pureed tomato mixture into your pot and cook the mixture down
  6. Once your onion and puree mixture is dark and caramelized, add a tablespoon of masala, mixing well
  7. Once the cooked spice and puree mixture is cooked down and caramelized, add in your water or stock
  8. Let the mixture rehydrate and mix it all together to create your "gravy"
  9. Add in your chickpeas and stew the mixture on low heat for a 5 to 10 minutes with the lid on
  10. Serve with warm pita, rice or by itself in a bowl!
  11. Optionally: squeeze lime and sprinkle freshly chopped cilantro on top
