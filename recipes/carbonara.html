Carbonara

A Brief Description of Carbonara:

Carbonara is an old Sicilian dish.

Egg yolks give the sauce a luxurious texture that you can't really replicate elsewise.

Ingredients:

  • Your Preferred Pasta Style (Fettucine is depicted)
  • Pancetta or Guanciale (or bacon in a pinch!)
  • Parmesan or Pecorino Romano
  • Black Pepper
  • Egg
  • Salt

Steps to create the dish:

  1. Bring water to a boil with a half tablespoon or so of salt added to it
  2. Once water is boiling, add your noodles and cook until preferred texture
  3. Remove your garlic from its skin and mince it finely
  4. Grate your chosen cheese as finely as your grater allows
  5. Dice your meat into small chunks, reseve some pieces to keep large to top your pasta with
  6. Heat a sautee pan on medium heat and add your preferred oil or butter
  7. Once appropriately heated, toss in your chosen meat and sautee until it starts to brown and oil is released
  8. In a bowl, separate two egg yolks from the whites
  9. Into your yolks, crack a healthy amount of fresh black pepper and add a bit of salt and your finely grated cheese, reserving some cheese for garnish
  10. Stir the yolk, pepper and cheese until combined
  11. Once your meat has browned to your preference, remove the larger chunks and set aside
  12. Add your cooked noodles into the pan and let everything warm up
  13. Once warm, kill the heat and pull the pan aside and add in your egg yolk miixture
  14. It will look thick at first but work it around, adding in a spoonful or two of your pasta water
  15. Once your carbonara sauce comes together in a silky smooth coating, transfer to a plate, top with reserved meat and garnish with reserved cheese!
